Service · Rescue

When the NetSuite implementation is on fire.

Milestones missed. Users frustrated. Executives losing patience. A rescue is what happens before the project gets cancelled or the partner gets fired. We come in fast, tell the truth, and ship something that works.

Signals you need a rescue

  • Go-live has slipped twice — and the new date isn't credible either
  • The current partner is "almost done" but can't show working transactions end-to-end
  • UAT is uncovering issues faster than they're being closed
  • Custom records and SuiteScript have multiplied beyond what anyone can explain
  • Finance is preparing to run another month on the legacy system "just in case"

How a rescue runs

  • Day 1–5 — Honest assessment. We map what's built, what works, what's broken, and what's missing. Written, blunt, with options.
  • Week 2 — Reset scope. We cut every feature that isn't go-live critical. Phase 2 is a real plan, not a graveyard.
  • Weeks 3–N — Ship. Working transactions, daily standups, visible burn-down. A new go-live date you can defend to the board.
  • Stabilize. Two weeks of hyper-care after go-live. Then we transition to a sustainment model — your team, our backup.
We don't replace your partner unless we have to. Often the right move is to stay with the implementer and add eMerge as an independent technical lead. We'll tell you which path is honest.

Need help today?

Rescue starts with a 60-minute call. Tell us the situation. If we're not the right fit, we'll say so on that call.
