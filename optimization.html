Service · Optimization

Make NetSuite faster, cheaper, and less manual.

Once the platform is stable, we look for ROI: workflows that should be automated, scripts that should be rewritten in 2.1, saved searches that should be Workbooks, and integrations that should be ODBC instead of polled.

Where we usually find wins

  • SuiteScript 2.1 modernization — async/await, better error handling, lower governance footprint than 1.0 holdovers.
  • Workflow consolidation — collapse overlapping SuiteFlows that fight each other on the same record.
  • Saved Searches → Workbooks — better performance, charting, and reusability for SuiteAnalytics.
  • ODBC / SuiteAnalytics Connect tuning — secure, indexed, and properly cached for BI tools (Power BI, Tableau).
  • Manual ops automation — month-end close, intercompany journals, vendor onboarding flows.

How we measure success

  • Time saved per week, per role (we baseline on day 1 and re-measure)
  • Saved-search and Workbook execution times (target: under 5 seconds for the daily ones)
  • Integration error rate (target: under 0.5%)
  • Number of SuiteScript 1.0 customizations retired
Optimization isn't a re-platform. We optimize your NetSuite — same account, same data, fewer headaches. No big-bang re-implementations.
